Our Story

Born in Madang,
made with love

PanuGirl started as a small passion project in Madang, Papua New Guinea — a vision to celebrate the rich colours, textures, and traditions of PNG through wearable art.

Founded by a local creative with a deep love for island culture, PanuGirl handcrafts every item with care. From vibrant fabric shirts inspired by tropical blooms to shell jewellery shaped by the sea — each piece tells a story.

Today, PanuGirl proudly serves customers across Madang and ships nationwide. We are a small business with a big heart, and your support helps us grow our community.

Our Policies

📦 Shipping Policy

  • Free delivery within Madang town (1–2 business days)
  • Nationwide delivery via Air Niugini or courier — fees apply depending on location
  • Orders are processed within 1–2 business days after payment confirmation
  • You will receive a notification once your order has been dispatched

🔄 Returns & Exchange Policy

  • Items can be exchanged within 7 days of purchase if unworn and in original condition
  • Custom orders are non-refundable once production has begun
  • If you received a damaged or incorrect item, contact us within 48 hours and we'll make it right
  • Sale items are final and cannot be exchanged

💳 Payment Policy

  • We accept cash on delivery (Madang only), bank transfer (BSP), and mobile money
  • Full payment is required before dispatch for online orders
  • Custom orders require a 50% deposit upfront
